# NeuroGym Focus Test - Demo Video Script

## Recording Setup
- **Tool**: QuickTime (Mac), OBS Studio, or Loom
- **Duration**: 2-3 minutes
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 recommended

## Script Timeline

### Scene 1: Introduction (0:00-0:15)
**Screen**: Landing page at https://kevin-msba.github.io/neurogym-focus-test/

**Narration**:
"In today's digital world, we constantly switch between apps, scroll through feeds, and lose focus. 
NeuroGym helps you measure and improve your cognitive performance."

**Action**: Hover over the "Start the 30-Second Focus Test" button

---

### Scene 2: Test Instructions (0:15-0:30)
**Action**: Click "Start the 30-Second Focus Test"

**Narration**:
"The test is simple. You'll see numbers and letters. Press LEFT for numbers, RIGHT for letters. 
But here's the challenge - the rules will change during the test."

**Action**: Click "I Understand" button

---

### Scene 3: Countdown (0:30-0:35)
**Screen**: 3-2-1 countdown with rules displayed

**Narration**:
"Get ready... the test begins now."

**Action**: Wait for countdown to complete

---

### Scene 4: Playing the Game (0:35-1:05)
**Action**: Play the game, responding to symbols
- Show correct responses (green checkmarks)
- Show some incorrect responses (red X marks)
- Let the rule switch happen mid-game

**Narration**:
"As you respond, NeuroGym measures your reaction time, accuracy, and how well you adapt when rules change. 
This simulates real-world focus challenges like switching between tasks."

---

### Scene 5: Results Screen (1:05-1:45)
**Screen**: Report screen with scores

**Narration**:
"After 30 seconds, you get your Focus Stability Score - a measure of your cognitive performance.


The Switching Cost shows how much slower you are when rules change - just like switching between apps.

Daily Focus Loss estimates how much productivity you lose to context switching."

**Action**: Scroll through the report slowly, highlighting each metric

---

### Scene 6: Future Vision (1:45-2:15)
**Screen**: Stay on report, focus on "How NeuroGym Helps" section

**Narration**:
"Imagine this: You're working on your computer, rapidly switching apps and scrolling through social media.

NeuroGym detects the pattern and shows a prompt: 'Your focus stability is dropping.'

At that moment, the system intervenes - suggesting a 60-second focus reset or triggering 
a short adaptive cognitive exercise designed to stabilize your attention.

Over time, you begin to see measurable improvements in your focus stability and recovery time."

**Action**: Hover over "Download NeuroGym" button

---

### Scene 7: Call to Action (2:15-2:30)
**Screen**: Click "Try Again" to show the cycle

**Narration**:
"NeuroGym turns cognitive science into actionable insights. 
Try the test yourself and discover how stable your attention really is."

**Action**: Show landing page again briefly

**End Screen**: Display URL: kevin-msba.github.io/neurogym-focus-test

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Use a clean browser window** - Close unnecessary tabs
2. **Disable notifications** - Turn on Do Not Disturb mode
3. **Practice first** - Do a test run to get comfortable
4. **Speak clearly** - Use a good microphone if possible
5. **Add background music** - Subtle, professional music (optional)
